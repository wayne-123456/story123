Scene 1: Qiu Yifeng’s Adventure Begins

Qiu Yifeng is a curious boy who carries a small backpack every day, ready for a new adventure.

Scene 2: Meeting the Little Cat

Qiu Yifeng meets a little cat in the park. It looks scared, and Qiu Yifeng decides to help it.

Scene 3: Building a Friendship

Qiu Yifeng gently picks up the little cat and takes it to find food and water. The little cat feels Qiu Yifeng’s kindness and begins to trust him.

Scene 4: Learning to Share

Qiu Yifeng takes the little cat home and shares snacks with his family. He learns the importance of sharing.

One day, the little cat gets lost, and Qiu Yifeng decides to use a map to find it. Along the way, he faces challenges but refuses to give up.

Scene 6: Qiu Yifeng’s Courage

Qiu Yifeng climbs a small hill and eventually finds the little cat from a higher vantage point. He is happy because he overcame his fear.

Scene 7: Returning Home

Qiu Yifeng finally brings the little cat home, and everyone is proud of him. He realizes that he has become braver.

Scene 8: New Friends

In the park, Qiu Yifeng meets more little animals and starts building friendships with them. He learns more about friendship and caring for others.

Scene 9: The Power of Teamwork

One day, the little cat brings more animals, and together they help Qiu Yifeng complete a big challenge. He learns the importance of cooperation and teamwork.

Scene 10: The End of the Adventure

At the end of the story, Qiu Yifeng sits on a park bench, reflecting on his adventure. His heart is filled with satisfaction and pride. He knows that no matter what challenges lie ahead, he won’t give up because he has learned to be brave, share, and care for others.
